Microsoft Office


3. To shrink an Excel file size: save it as .xslb.
4. To open your PowerPoint file in presentation mode directly: Right click on the file, then select Show.


5. To use mouse as laser pointer in PowerPoint: hold Ctrl + Left click. Mouse cursors are too mainstream. Stay cool with laser.

YouTube


6. To pause a video: Press K.
7. To skip 10 seconds back and forward: Press J and L respectively.
8. To search videos from a specific user only: Type @ before you search.
9. To save your time watching videos: Press > and < to speed up and slow down the video respectively.
10. To view all keyboard shortcuts: Press ?.

11. To boost your Wi-Fi signal: Download Virtual Router to turn your computer(s) into a Wi-Fi hotspot. It will extend the reach of your network.

Google Crome


13. To open Chrome’s built-in task manager: Press Shift + Esc. Extremely useful when Chrome freezes.


14. To drag multiple tabs to a new window: Press Ctrl + Click on tabs you want to move. Do the same to move multiple tabs to an existing window.


15. To scroll horizontally: Press Shift + Scroll.

Any Browser


16. To access your address bar directly: Press F6 or Ctrl + L.
